Animated Little Mouse Flannelboard

An animated version of the popular storytime game, Little Mouse, made with JavaScript. Traditional flannelboard version here.

As children choose a house to look under, click on that house. It will be greyed out if there is no mouse underneath. A pop-up displays the mouse when the children find it. Repeat as often as the children wish!
